Oh Hello,

I'm Jeremy, a designer based in Madison, WI with a knack for creating accessible and efficient design systems. I have over a decade of experience leading brand design for ambitious startups and industry-defining tech companies.

Currently, I'm an Art Director at Chess.com, where we're creating the best place to play with the largest chess community on the internet.

Before that, I was Design Director for EatStreet, where my naturally curious nature flourished to meet the ever-changing demands of a diy startup culture.

When not designing, you can catch me cheering on the Packers, watching Jeopardy with my wife, and making ridiculous faces at my son.
